Cactus and Succulent Care

As easy as 1, 2, 3!

1) Location

Place your cactus or succulent in a location with ample natural light. A windowsill or bed stand next to a window is perfect.

2) Water

Water your succulent thoroughly once a week, adding enough water that you see water pool in the dish below (make sure to remove the pooled water!. If you forget every now and then, don’t worry! Just don’t wait more than 2 weeks between watering. Try to remember to keep the soil at least somewhat moist at all times. This may mean watering once a week but could be as little as once a month depending on the season and humidity of your dorm room.

3) Rotate

Every so often (about once every 2 weeks), rotate your plant about 90 degrees to make sure that it’s getting an equal distribution of light.

Enjoy the vibrancy and resiliency of your succulent!
