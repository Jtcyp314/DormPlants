About Us

Dorm Plant is a subscription service designed for the lifestyle and budget of college students. We offer easy to take care of plants that bring a refreshing pop of color to a room. Our mission is to create a simple way for people to incorporate plants into their daily life because plants make our lives happier and our minds healthier.

Frequently Asked Questions

To sign up for a plant subscription, please indicate the type of plant you would like (Succulent/Cacti or Ivy/Spider Plant) as well as the frequency (bi-weekly, monthly, bi-monthly). Based on your purchase date, we will ship you a random plant with customized care instructions.
Any changes to your subscription plan should be made before your plan renews. Otherwise, your plan will stay the same and you will be charged until the following month.
Allow 5-10 business days from your purchase date to receive your delivery. Your subscription will renew automatically on your purchase date, and your next box will ship out.
Oh no! If an item arrives damaged or is missing from your box, send an email to Dorm Plant Customer Support (help@DormPlant.com) within 7 days of delivery and we will ship you a replacement item at no cost to you.
Due to the nature of our products, we can only issue refunds and accept returns in the unfortunate event that your delivery arrives damaged.
